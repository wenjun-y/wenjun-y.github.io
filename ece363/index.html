Communication Networks

(ECE363)


Overview: Introduction to communication networking principles and engineering including resource management, error control, flow control, congestion control, TCP/IP model, algorithm and protocol design for wireless and wired communication networks.


Prerequisites: CENG 255 or ECE 255 or CSC 230

Textbook:
